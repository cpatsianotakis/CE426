Something about me:

I was born in 23 September 1997 in Belgium. I have a Greek father and a Belgian mother and I grew up in Pombia, a village of the south of Crete. My father is an Electrical Engineer, as a result, I started to work on electric automation from the age of 12. Nowadays I live in the beautiful city of Volos and I am studying Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Thessaly. I like working mostly on Embedded Systems. Also I am an IEEE member and for this season (2019-2020) I am the Vice Chair of the Student Branch of University of Thessaly (Volos). I manage also (and started) the AgroJason Project, a project working on the Greenhouses automation. I do also fencing, and as a teenager I've got the 2nd and 3rd place in Greece. Also I played in the Junior World Cup which was held in Thessaloniki in 2014. Nowadays I am a National Referee of the Hellenic Federation of Fencing and I work also as a coach, focusing on developing the sport in Thessaly.
